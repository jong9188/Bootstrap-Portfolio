About Me

Jonathan Glincman grew up in North Brunswick, NJ. Since childhood, he remained active in athletics, competing in three sports during high school. He attended TCNJ for undergraduate studies, and was a member of the tennis team. Upon graduation with a Marketing degree, Jonathan pursued sales at Liberty Mutual. During his tenure there, he attended and completed graduate studies at Seton Hall University while working full time. He has continued to actively participate in sports, while developing new skills and knowledge.
